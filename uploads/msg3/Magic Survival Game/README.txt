================================================================================
                    MAGIC SURVIVAL - PLAYER GUIDE
================================================================================

WELCOME!

Magic Survival is a fun magic battle game where you fight off waves of enemies
and try to survive for 15 minutes to defeat the Final Boss!

================================================================================
                         HOW TO PLAY
================================================================================

1. Choose your hero (Wizard, Knight, or Archer)
2. Move around with W, A, S, D keys
3. Kill enemies to level up
4. At 15 minutes, the Final Boss appears - defeat it to WIN!

================================================================================
                         CONTROLS
================================================================================

MOVEMENT:
  W, A, S, D - Move around

GAME SPEED (Speed up or slow down the game):
  LEFT ARROW  - Slow down
  RIGHT ARROW - Speed up
  DOWN ARROW  - Normal speed

MENUS:
  I - Open your stats and ability menu
  R - Restart game after you die or win

================================================================================
                         ABILITIES
================================================================================

You start with basic attacks. As you level up, you can unlock two special powers:

FIREBREATH - Shoot fire everywhere around you
  - Gets stronger with each level (up to level 7)
  - Good for clearing groups of enemies

LIGHTNING - Strike enemies with lightning bolts
  - Chains between multiple enemies
  - Gets more powerful and hits more enemies as you level up

================================================================================
                         LEVELING UP
================================================================================

When you level up, choose to upgrade:
  - Your Max Health
  - Attack Speed
  - Move Speed
  - Firebreath Power
  - Lightning Power

Each upgrade makes your character stronger. Pick what you need!

================================================================================
                         THE FINAL BOSS
================================================================================

At 15 minutes, a purple boss appears with 8 glowing eyes.

The boss:
  - Shoots purple orbs at you (dodge them!)
  - Gets stronger the longer you fight
  - Defeated when its health reaches zero

Tips:
  - Keep moving to avoid the orbs
  - Use your abilities at full power
  - Lightning chains across the arena
  - You win when the boss dies!

================================================================================
                    DIFFICULTY ADJUSTMENT
================================================================================

Press I to open your inventory. You'll see a "Difficulty Multiplier" slider.

Use LEFT and RIGHT arrows to adjust:
  - Lower = Fewer enemies (easier)
  - Higher = More enemies (harder)

This is great for testing the game at different difficulty levels!

================================================================================
                         CREATIVE MODE
================================================================================

For testing/debug mode, press C to enable Creative Mode.

In Creative Mode:
  - Press M to instantly unlock all abilities and skip to the boss
  - Press B to spawn extra enemies to fight

This is for testing only!

================================================================================
                         HAVE FUN!
================================================================================

That's all you need to know. Just run the game and enjoy!

Good luck defeating the Final Boss!
