BrewLine — Smart Coffee Capsule Factory

A simulated end‑to‑end manufacturing line for single‑serve coffee capsules. Five animated production stages, an industrial SCADA HMI, defect classifier, CMMS health management and a time‑series analytics layer that streams to InfluxDB & Grafana.
