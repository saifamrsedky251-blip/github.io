InfluxDB integration

Metrics are written to an InfluxDB v2 bucket using line protocol. The Python simulator (/simulation/influx_writer.py) emits one point per stage per second.

brewline,line=A,stage=fill capsules=12i,defects=0i,temp=92.4,pressure=4.31 1709321400000000000
brewline,line=A,stage=seal capsules=12i,defects=1i,temp=94.1,pressure=4.42 1709321401000000000

bucket: brewline · org: university · retention: 30d

Grafana dashboard

A provisioning JSON is included at /dashboard/grafana_dashboard.json. Import it into your Grafana instance and point its data‑source at your InfluxDB bucket.

  • Panel 1 — Throughput (capsules / min)
  • Panel 2 — Pass vs Reject (stacked)
  • Panel 3 — Sealer temperature (gauge + history)
  • Panel 4 — Piston pressure
  • Panel 5 — Machine health (5 stat panels)
  • Panel 6 — Alarm timeline (table)
