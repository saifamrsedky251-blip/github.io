Powers

Two abilities. Seven levels each.

Pick them up from the level‑up menu. They scale dramatically — level 7 is a different game.

🔥

Firebreath

Cone of fire particles around you

  • Damage scales ×3 at level 7
  • Triple emission rate & 5× range at max level
  • Cooldown drops from 3.0s → 1.8s
⚡

Lightning

Chains across multiple enemies in 500 px range

  • Chains to 2 + level additional targets
  • Width & damage scale every 3 levels
  • Level 7 — ×2.2 damage, double bolt width

Endgame

The 8‑eyed Final Boss.

At the 15‑minute mark, the arena darkens. A purple horror with eight glowing eyes appears and starts hurling cyan orbs across the screen. Dodge, weave, chain your lightning, and unload everything you've got — the run ends when one of you stops moving.

  • Keep moving — orbs are slow but relentless.
  • Lightning chains shred adds during the boss phase.
  • Boss damage scales the longer you fight — don't stall.
